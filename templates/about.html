About the System

Modern manufacturing industries consume significant energy and produce high carbon emissions. This AI-driven platform monitors industrial batch processes, predicts energy usage and carbon footprint, and provides actionable insights to optimize efficiency and sustainability.

- Carbon emission estimation
- Predictive analytics for batch optimization
- Resource utilization and efficiency improvement

How to Use

2. Monitor live batch parameters
3. View AI-predicted energy consumption and carbon emission
4. Download reports and optimize operations
